About Blood Donation

Blood donation is the voluntary process of giving blood that can be used for transfusions or made into medicines.

Why Blood Donation Matters?

  • Saves lives during emergencies.
  • Supports surgeries and medical treatments.
  • Helps accident victims.
  • Supports cancer patients.
  • Assists women during childbirth complications.

Benefits of Blood Donation

  1. Helps save lives.
  2. Improves blood circulation.
  3. Reduces harmful iron stores.
  4. Provides free health screening.
  5. Promotes community service.
  6. Creates social responsibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Healthy individuals aged 18-65 years can donate blood.

How often can I donate?

Every 3 months for whole blood donation.

Is blood donation safe?

Yes, blood donation is completely safe when performed under medical supervision.
